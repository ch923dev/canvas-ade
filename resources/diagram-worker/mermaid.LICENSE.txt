Why vendored (not an npm dependency)
------------------------------------
Mermaid is loaded by the HIDDEN diagram render worker (a Chromium BrowserWindow,
src/main/diagramWorker.ts) via a plain <script> tag in worker.html — NOT imported into
the app bundle. Vendoring the self-contained UMD/IIFE build keeps it out of the renderer/
main Vite graph, avoids pulling Mermaid's heavy transitive deps (d3 / dagre / cytoscape)
into the shared workspace install, and lets the worker run fully offline under a locked
`default-src 'none'` CSP (the single-file build performs zero dynamic chunk imports).

Updating
--------

e2e @planning diagram spec covers this) before committing a bump.
