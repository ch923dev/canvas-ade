E · the contract (what this revision locks)

mic gate — structural

Open panel = mic may listen. Closed panel = mic cannot. Converse arms only from the open panel; close/Esc runs the same teardown as converse-off today (stop capture, cancel turn, drop queued speech). If you can’t see the conversation, it can’t hear you.

wake word (J5) carve-out

The local KeywordSpotter (no cloud, no STT) may run with the panel closed, opt-in only, and its sole power is to OPEN the panel. Turns still require the open panel.

cost posture

Dictation (VoicePill) stays free/local and untouched. Jarvis is opt-in behind the Persona toggle; no island means no paid affordance on screen for users who never enable it.

what retires / what carries

Retired: floating island, floating tail. Carried unchanged: brain, IPC + turn lifecycle, clause chunker, barge-in seams, stores, PersonaPane, unit + e2e coverage (asserts are store-level).
