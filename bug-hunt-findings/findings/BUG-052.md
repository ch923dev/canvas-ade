# BUG-052: Transient drag positions (and alt-drag ghost copies) of a checklist permanently grow the board height via the untracked growBoardHeight path

- **Severity:** Low
- **Category:** data-integrity
- **Status:** CONFIRMED
- **Files touched:** `src/renderer/src/canvas/boards/PlanningBoard.tsx`, `src/renderer/src/canvas/boards/planning/ChecklistCard.tsx`
- **Roadmap:** none
- **Assigned:** _(left blank for the dev team)_

## Summary
During a drag, viewElements renders the moving checklist at its transient shifted y (movedView, line 509), and alt-drags additionally mount a ghost ChecklistCard at the shifted position (lines 514-519). ChecklistCard's measure effect re-runs on every element.y change (ChecklistCard.tsx:111-122 deps include element.y) and reports element.y + offsetHeight; growForChecklist then calls useCanvasStore.getState().growBoardHeight(board.id, needed) whenever needed > board.h — a store mutation that is deliberately untracked (never undoable) and only-grows. So merely dragging a checklist toward the board's bottom — even if the gesture is cancelled (pointercancel), released with zero net movement (no commit), or is just a ghost preview of an alt-duplicate that ends elsewhere — permanently and un-undoably grows the persisted board height, which then autosaves into canvas.json. The grow is meant to track committed content, but it fires from preview-only render state.

## Where
`src/renderer/src/canvas/boards/PlanningBoard.tsx:283` — `growForChecklist` (useCallback inside the `PlanningBoard` component; the `growBoardHeight` call)

## How it triggers
Grab a checklist card on a planning board, drag it near/past the bottom edge, then drag back up and release at the original spot (or hit Esc / OS pointer-cancel mid-drag, or alt-drag a ghost downward and release elsewhere). The board is now permanently taller; undo does nothing (growBoardHeight is untracked by design).

## Verification evidence
Confirmed mechanical path. During a drag, viewElements renders the moving checklist at its transient shifted y (PlanningBoard.tsx:509 movedView via translateMany, 521-526), and an alt-drag additionally mounts a ghost ChecklistCard (514-519) with the full prop set including onMeasureBottom={growForChecklist} (701-718). ChecklistCard's measure effect has element.y in its deps (ChecklistCard.tsx:122) and reports element.y + offsetHeight (115), so every drag frame that moves the card down re-fires it. growForChecklist (PlanningBoard.tsx:276-286) ignores the id (`_elId`) — ghost ids included — and calls useCanvasStore.getState().growBoardHeight(board.id, needed) whenever needed > board.h. growBoardHeight (canvasStore.ts:639-645, doc 174-179) is deliberately UNTRACKED (never touches undo/redo) and only-grows, and board.h is serialized → autosaved into canvas.json. Nothing distinguishes committed from transient/ghost positions, so a cancelled drag (onWellPointerCancel routes move-mode to onWellPointerUp with dragPos cleared — no element commit), a zero-net-movement release, or a ghost preview that ends elsewhere all leave the board permanently taller with no undo. Recoverable only by manually resizing the board → Low.

## Suggested fix direction
Suppress growForChecklist while dragPos is non-null (or for __ghost__ ids), growing only from committed positions.
