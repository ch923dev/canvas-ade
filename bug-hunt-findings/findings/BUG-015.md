# BUG-015: eraseHitTest uses a fixed 160×24 nominal box for text — XL/area text largely un-erasable/un-targetable; short text has a phantom hit band

- **Severity:** Medium
- **Category:** correctness
- **Status:** CONFIRMED
- **Files touched:** `src/renderer/src/canvas/boards/planning/erase.ts`, `src/renderer/src/canvas/boards/planning/usePlanningPointer.ts`, `src/renderer/src/canvas/boards/planning/textStyle.ts`
- **Roadmap:** none
- **Assigned:** _(left blank for the dev team)_

## Summary
eraseHitTest's text case is `inRect(p, el.x, el.y, TEXT_HIT.w, TEXT_HIT.h, tol)` with TEXT_HIT hardcoded to {w:160,h:24}, ignoring el.width, el.fontSize, and the live DOM measurement (measuredRef is threaded into marquee/snap but not into erase or the right-click hit scan). Since schema v7 typography, an XL multi-line or wide area text is only hittable in its top-left 160×24(+8) corner — eraser swipes and right-clicks over the rest do nothing — while a short S-size text carries an invisible 160×24 hit band, so an erase swipe through visually empty space right of it deletes it and right-click in that band targets it instead of what is under the cursor.

## Where
`src/renderer/src/canvas/boards/planning/erase.ts:100` — `eraseHitTest` (the `case 'text'` branch at erase.ts:100-101)

## How it triggers
Create an XL multi-line or area text (drag with the text tool); switch to the eraser and swipe over its lower lines or right half — nothing erases. Or right-click the lower half of the text — no context menu. Or erase-swipe ~100px to the right of a short text — it is deleted.

## Verification evidence
Confirmed. erase.ts:32 hardcodes TEXT_HIT={w:160,h:24} and the text case (erase.ts:100-101) is inRect(p, el.x, el.y, TEXT_HIT.w, TEXT_HIT.h, tol) — el.width (area text, persisted by makeText, elements.ts:74-80, created at usePlanningPointer.ts:466-473 with width up to the drag box) and fontSize (SIZE_PX XL=26, lineHeight 36 — textStyle.ts:55-57) are ignored, and eraseHitTest's signature (erase.ts:92) cannot accept the measured map. All three call sites pass only (el, p): usePlanningPointer.ts:241, :307 (erase down/move) and :348 (onWellContextMenu target scan). Meanwhile the SAME elements report exact DOM sizes into measuredRef, consumed by marquee/snap only (usePlanningPointer.ts:282/288/441; FreeText onMeasure wired at PlanningBoard.tsx:694). So a 500px-wide area text or multi-line XL heading is only hittable in its top-left 160×24(+8) corner (eraser swipes and right-clicks over the rest do nothing), while a ~40px one-liner carries an invisible ~128px-wide phantom erase/target band to its right. erase.test.ts:21-24 pins the nominal box (pre-v7 behavior) — it doesn't contradict the claim; the erase.ts:30 comment ('W2 refines this with a DOM-measured bbox') shows the refinement was intended and never landed for erase.

## Suggested fix direction
Thread the measured map (or at least el.width + fontSize-derived height) into eraseHitTest's text case, mirroring elementBBox's text branch.
