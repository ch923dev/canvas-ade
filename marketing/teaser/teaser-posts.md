## LinkedIn

Attach: `expanse-teaser-landscape.png`

---

Working with AI coding agents today means living in chaos.

One terminal for the agent. A browser to check the preview. A notes app for the plan. Ten tabs you've lost track of. The work is connected — your tools aren't.

I'm building Expanse to fix that: an infinite, zoomable surface where each piece of your work is a board in one place.

→ A terminal board runs a real coding agent in a real shell.
→ A browser board shows your localhost app live, in a device frame.
→ A planning board holds your notes, arrows and checklists right next to the code.

No tabs. No window juggling. Zoom out to see the whole project; zoom in to do the work.

A calm, local-first desktop app — designed for focus, not noise.

Early access is coming soon. If "one infinite surface for your agents" sounds like something you'd use, follow along and I'll share the first real look shortly.

What does your AI-dev workspace look like right now? Curious if I'm the only one drowning in tabs. 👇
